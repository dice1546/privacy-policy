Privacy Policy for 24 Hours Radio

Welcome to 24 Hours Radio! This privacy policy governs your use of the software application 24 Hours Radio (“App”) for mobile devices that was created by 24 Hours Radio Ltd..

About 24 Hours Radio

24 Hours Radio is a web broadcast radio that primarily streams music and everyday life-related content. The App is designed for users aged 18 and above. Our content does not include any explicit material, and we do not aim to appeal to children. We are committed to providing a safe and enjoyable listening experience for our audience.

Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect any personal data from its users. The only data collected is through Google Analytics, which is used solely to analyze user activity in order to improve the quality of the App. This may include data such as how often users visit the App, what actions they take within the App, and other related metrics.

We do not share any of this data with third parties, and the information is used exclusively for the purpose of enhancing your experience with the App.
